ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Bradley Hobbs

Bradley Hobbs writes stories, devotionals, and faith centered journeys that explore healing, grace, identity, restoration, and the life changing presence of Jesus. Through symbolic storytelling and heartfelt reflections, his work creates spaces where difficult experiences can meet hope and where hidden places can encounter truth.

Many stories grow from themes of church hurt, healing, surrender, personal growth, and discovering the voice of Jesus in seasons filled with silence and uncertainty. Each work seeks to remind readers that broken places do not disqualify anyone from receiving grace.

The House That Welcomed Him continues that journey through the story of Elias, revealing how homes can become reflections of the heart and how transformation begins through honesty, trust, and preparing a dwelling place for the Spirit.

“Every house reflects the heart.”
