The House That Welcomed Him

A symbolic journey through cleansing, trust, and preparing the heart as a dwelling place for the Spirit.

Contents

Follow Elias through four stages of transformation. Every room reveals more than clutter. Every chapter uncovers what happens when a heart becomes a place where peace is welcome.

CHAPTER ONE

The House in Disorder

Elias encounters a traveler whose arrival reveals a painful truth. Disorder inside the home mirrors disorder hidden within the heart.

CHAPTER TWO

The Work Begins

Old fears, buried regret, and hidden wounds rise into the light as Elias begins a difficult process of cleansing and surrender.

CHAPTER THREE

The Spirit Dwells

Peace gradually replaces heaviness as transformation begins shaping both the home and the life inside.

CHAPTER FOUR

A Home Transformed

The once neglected dwelling becomes a sanctuary where love, peace, and daily devotion create a place of welcome.
