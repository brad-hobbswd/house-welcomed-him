A neglected house becomes a living picture of the human heart. Through the arrival of a mysterious traveler, Elias begins a journey of cleansing, surrender, trust, and transformation.

About This Story

The House That Welcomed Him follows Elias through forgotten rooms filled with fear, regret, pride, and buried pain. A traveler arrives and reveals a difficult truth. Every house reflects the heart. Through each room and every chapter, readers experience a symbolic journey toward renewal and preparing a dwelling place for the Spirit.

“Every house reflects the heart.”
