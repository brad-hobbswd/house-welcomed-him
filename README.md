## About The Book

*The House That Welcomed Him* follows Elias through a deeply symbolic journey of cleansing and renewal.

Years of neglect left more than physical clutter inside his home. Hidden rooms reflected buried fears, regret, pride, and pain carried quietly through life. Everything changes when a mysterious traveler arrives and reveals a truth Elias had never fully understood:

Every house reflects the heart.

Room by room, the story unfolds through honesty, surrender, and daily transformation as Elias learns that preparing a dwelling place for the Spirit begins from within.
