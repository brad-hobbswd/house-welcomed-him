THE JOURNEY CONTINUES

Closing Reflections

The House That Welcomed Him began with disorder, heaviness, and rooms long neglected. Elias discovered that the condition of his home reflected something deeper. Every forgotten space pointed toward hidden burdens within the heart.

Through cleansing, honesty, surrender, and daily care, transformation slowly unfolded. Change did not arrive through perfection. Growth came through willingness. Room by room, space was prepared for peace.

Many homes contain hidden rooms. Many hearts carry forgotten places. Fear, pride, regret, bitterness, and pain often remain tucked behind closed doors.

The invitation remains the same. Open the door. Welcome healing. Choose what nurtures life. Continue preparing a dwelling place where the Spirit is welcome.

“Every house reflects the heart.”
